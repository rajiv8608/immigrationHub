I-9 and E-Verify Compliance Solution:

Form I-9 and E-verify are the crucial part of any business on boarding procedure. It is the employment Eligibility verification and it is one of the Immigration services forms. Federal law demands employers to verify the employment authorization and identity. One has to show their employers documents from the list of acceptable documents. E-verify is basically an internet-based system which can compare the information from an employees' form I9 to data from the U.S. Department of Homeland Security and Social Security Administration records to authenticate the service.

H1 and H2 Visas:

H1B visa is a predominant work visa for professionals and students from in the USA or all over the world. It's a temporary permit for travel or to work in the US in a specialty occupation. Under this visa, employers in the United States can work temporarily in companies. These professionals must hold a bachelors� degree or something equivalent. US H1B visa remains for an initial period of 3 years and valid up to 6 years. H2B visa is a temporary for short-term or we can say for seasonal work being it as hospitality or tourist industries. H1B is more prominent than H2B.

B1 and B2:

Business (B1) Visa is a non-immigrant visa to the USA. One who would like to travel to the US for short duration for business associated work like conferences, meetings. Investors who come to the United States to form their investments, Employees of a company who come to the United States to negotiate contracts, to solicit sales opportunities or investment, or take orders from established clients for work to be performed outside the United States. But, one should not eligible to conduct meetings or earnings under the B1 visa. B2 is a tourist visa it includes tourism, visit friends or relatives or medical treatment or Participation in musical, sports or similar events as amateur.

L1A:

L-1 visa is a temporary transfer of foreign worker in the executive, specialized or in the managerial category to the US to peruse his services with an office of the same employer, its branch, or subsidiary. The purpose of L1 visa is to permit large multinationals - with operations across several countries - to shift employees in specialized positions on a temporary basis to the United States. Initially for a L1A visa holder have three year period extendible in two year increments to a maximum of seven years. However, if people under the L1A visa may apply for a permanent resident status, they will be granted this status within a year of filing.

Perm/GC:

Green Card is a permanent resident card which allows you to live and work in the United States. L1 Visa holders are eligible to apply for a green cards under EBIC category. Green Card is valid for lifetime. PERM is the procedure for obtaining labor certification, the first step of the green card process for foreign nationals seeking permanent residence through their employment. Not only through employment, can one apply green card through family, Registry.
